Privacy Policy for Jeda

Last Updated: March 3, 2025

Thank you for using Jeda, the holiday calendar app designed to help you plan and manage your holidays efficiently. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to being transparent about how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Jeda does not collect, store, or share any personal information. However, the app may access certain device identifiers for limited functionality, as detailed below.

2. Advertising ID Usage

Jeda may access your device’s Advertising ID (provided by Google Play services) for analytics, app improvement, and potential ad-related functionality in the future. This identifier is used in accordance with Google’s Advertising ID policy. You can reset or disable it via your device settings.

3. Notification Permissions

Jeda may request permission to send notifications to your device. This permission is used solely to provide reminders and updates about holidays or events you have set in the app. Notifications are enabled only if you explicitly allow them, and you can disable them at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Jeda does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app functions independently without external data collection or processing.

5. Children's Privacy

Jeda is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal data, there is no risk of unintentional data collection from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If any changes are made, we will notify you through the app or via email. Your continued use of Jeda after any updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
